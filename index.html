Impact Mapping

A collaborative strategic planning technique

Context

The technology world need an approach that engages DEV organisations as full partner's with other disciplines

EU companies lost 142B Euros on failed IT projects in just one year (2014), like the cost of the international space station programm

Some Reasons: Poor alignment with business objectives, business strategies becoming obsolete during IT project delivery

Root Causes of IT failed projects

Wrong business assumptions

Lack of focus on the business goal

Poor communication of objectives

Lack of business understanding

Misalignment with overall goals

What is impact mapping?

A method for collaborative strategic planning

It provides guidance to help organisations make an impact on software

Benefits of impact mapping?

+ Better plans and product roadmaps

+ Clear communication of assumptions

Impact Map

Definition: A visualisation of scope and underlying assumptions

Answer questions: Why?, Who?, How?, What?

Why?

The goal we are trying to achieve

A goal is the explanation why such a thing would be useful

Definition of problem to be solved, not the solution

Knowing why we are doing something is the key to make good decisions about cost, scope and timelines

Benefits of clear goal communication?

Everyone knows why they are doing something

It helps teams to align their activities better

It helps identify true requirements and design better solutions

Starting to trade in Brazil by March next year
