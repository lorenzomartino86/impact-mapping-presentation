Impact Mapping

A collaborative strategic planning technique

Context

The technology world need an approach that engages DEV organisations as full partner's with other disciplines

EU companies lost 142B Euros on failed IT projects in just one year (2014), like the cost of the international space station programm

Some Reasons: Poor alignment with business objectives, business strategies becoming obsolete during IT project delivery

Root Causes of IT failed projects

Wrong business assumptions

Lack of focus on the business goal

Poor communication of objectives

Lack of business understanding

Misalignment with overall goals

What is impact mapping?

A method for collaborative strategic planning

It provides guidance to help organisations make an impact on software

Benefits of impact mapping?

+ Better plans and product roadmaps

+ Clear communication of assumptions

Impact Map

Definition: A visualisation of scope and underlying assumptions

Answer questions: Why?, Who?, How?, What?

Why?

The goal we are trying to achieve

A goal is the explanation why such a thing would be useful

Definition of problem to be solved, not the solution

Knowing why we are doing something is the key to make good decisions about cost, scope and timelines

Benefits of clear goal communication?

Everyone knows why they are doing something

It helps teams to align their activities better

It helps identify true requirements and design better solutions

Samples

Starting to trade in Brazil by March next year

Increasing user conversion rate by 20% in 3 months

Who?

The involved actors

Who can produce the desired effect to achieve the goal?

Who can obstruct the goal?

Who are the consumer of our products and who will be impacted by our goal?

Identify actors

Thinking about decision-makers, user groups and customer segments

Considering primary actors first

Samples

Paul Red from the marketing department

Under 18 users with a mobile device

How?

The expected impacts

How should our actors behaviour change?

Actors behaviour are impacts

Samples

Inviting more friends

Selling tickets faster

What?

The deliverables

What can we do to support the required impact?

Treat deliverables as options with no much details

Samples

on-line ticket sales

mobile home page with purchase form

Usage of Impact Map

Advise: Use impact map to find the shortest path through the map to the goal

Sample of Impact Map

Benefits of Impact Map

+ Communicate assumptions

+ Create plans

+ Align stakeholders

Apply Impact Map to strategic planning

You need strategic goals - impact mapping is not applicable to maintenance mode projects

You need the partecipation of senior decision-makers and senior technical staff

Apply Impact Map to define quality

You need agreement that the purpose of deliverables is to support changes in actor behaviour

You need agreement on metrics that express stakeholders expectations

Apply Impact Map to roadmap management

You need agreement that your aim is to achieve a business goal, not deliver pre-set scope

You need frequent, iterative releases to measure progress

You need agreement on metrics that express stakeholders expectations for the central business goal
